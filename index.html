About Us

Our College

National Post Graduate College was established in the year 1974 by the then Chief-minister of Uttar Pradesh, Late Chandra Bhanu Gupta. The college is situated close to the banks of river Gomti in the heart of the city has lush green campus and is surrounded by a number of major institutions - Academic and Administrative.Study centre for IGNOU and Uttar Pradesh Rajarshi Tandon Open University, Allahabad are offering a number of certificate, diploma and degree programs in distant mode.
